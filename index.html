Places must visit

Nahargarh Fort

I had visited in 2016

The fort has a very nice and breathtaking view of the city, it is situated on the hills so it makes it a lot more beautiful, the insides of the fort are exactly what you'd expect from an archaic building. The fort gives you an awesome look on the city in the daylight and in the night but it is quite unsafe to go there in the night due to different carnivores. I personally think that it isn't a place where you should go alone especially at night cause it might have its limitations but above all it is a place worth visiting. As it is a little far from the main city, you can book a vehicle to get here and trust me once you do, you won't regret it

Taj lake hotel Udaipur

I had visited in 2018

The experience we had can't be depicted in words or pictures... It was just wonderful !!! The staff is so cooperative and good that you fall in love with the place and the service The food is worth trying and is very mouth watering specially the icecream they serve. A must visit place for anyone looking for everything perfect✨

Kodaikanal Lake

I had visited in 2018

The boating experience was awesome, great weather and nominal rates for the boats. Ice Cream vans are available around the periphery of the lake, where you can enjoy ice creams while boating. Many shopping spots, small eateries, cycles for rent, horse riding available around the lake. Taking padaling boats is more fun.

Kalatop forest rest house Himachal Pradesh

I had visited in 2017

The entire Kalatop Forest House spread in 2/3 acres of land on a hill top surrounded by pine trees.....!! The temperature of this place is normally 4/5 degree centigrade lower than Dalhousie/Khajjiar.....!! The property comprises of two huts and one bungalow made woods and rocks....!! There are number of trekking routes starts from Kalatop like route to Khajjier (14 KM), Joth (15KM). Trained and approved guides are available in the rest house.....!!

Dalhousie

I had visited in 2016

Dalhousie is a mesmerizing place to visit in India situated in the Chamba District. Located at an altitude of 1970 meters above sea level, this destination is worth a visit for all those who seek peace and tranquillity.
